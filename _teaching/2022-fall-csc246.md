Teaching Assistant for CSC 246: Operating Systems at North Carolina State University.

## Course Description

CSC 246 covers the fundamental concepts of operating systems including:
- Process management and scheduling
- Memory management and virtual memory
- File systems and I/O systems
- Synchronization and concurrency
- Deadlocks and resource allocation

## Duration

Fall 2022 - Spring 2024 (Multiple semesters)

- Held office hours to assist students with course material and assignments
- Graded assignments, projects, and exams
- Assisted with laboratory sessions and debugging help
- Provided support for systems programming in C
